# Design DNA — Parcelab

## Visual Personality: bold-energetic
## Layout: community
## Typography: bold-display

### Color Palette
- Primary: #E8452A → oklch(0.56 0.19 28) — Parce Red
- Accent: #F5C842 → oklch(0.83 0.17 88) — Oro Antioqueño
- Surface (dark base): #1A1A2E → oklch(0.13 0.03 260) — Medellín Night
- Surface mid: oklch(0.18 0.03 260) — elevated dark
- Surface card: oklch(0.20 0.025 260) — card backgrounds
- Text primary: oklch(0.97 0.005 80) — warm off-white
- Text muted: oklch(0.70 0.015 80) — warm gray

### Font Pairing
- Display: Archivo Black (Google Fonts) — 900 weight, all-caps section headers, bold headlines
- Body: Barlow — 400/500/600 weights — readable, confident, clean

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Noise-textured dark hero** with offset warm red radial glow (top-left) + gold glow (bottom-right) — feels like warm firelight on a dark Medellín night, NOT cyberpunk neon
2. **Giant community stats** with oversized numerals in Parce Red as the section centerpiece — number as hero, not supporting text
3. **Asymmetric diagonal cut** between hero and stats sections using CSS clip-path — breaks the sterile grid feel
4. **Bold all-caps Archivo Black section headers** with a short Parce Red underline accent mark — city-pride energy
5. **How-it-works cards** with numbered step indicator in gold — visible, bold, human
